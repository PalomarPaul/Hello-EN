7. Writing para B1
Tu escritura debe ser clara, organizada y sencilla. Evita frases demasiado largas. Usa conectores.
7.1 Estructura de párrafo
Modelo
Topic sentence: presenta la idea.
Detail 1: primera razón o detalle.
Detail 2: segunda razón o ejemplo.
Closing sentence: cierre sencillo.

Example:
English is important for my future. First, it can help me understand more information online. Also, it can give me better opportunities at school and work. For that reason, I want to practice every day.
7.2 Emails básicos
Parte
Frases útiles
Saludo
Hi Ana, / Dear Mr. Smith,
Inicio
I hope you are well. / I am writing because...
Cuerpo
I wanted to ask about... / I would like to know... / Also,...
Cierre
Thank you for your help. / I look forward to your answer.
Despedida
Best regards, / Sincerely,
7.3 Writing tasks para practicar
* Write an email asking for information about an English course.
* Write a paragraph about your daily routine.
* Write about your last vacation or weekend.
* Write your opinion about technology in education.
* Write advice for a friend who wants to be healthier.
* Write about a goal you have for next year.
* Write a review of a restaurant, movie, or app.
7.4 Checklist antes de entregar writing
* [ ] Cada oración tiene sujeto y verbo.
* [ ] Usé mayúscula al inicio y punto al final.
* [ ] No mezclé presente y pasado sin razón.
* [ ] Agregué conectores: because, but, also, so.
* [ ] La idea principal se entiende sin traducir palabra por palabra.
* [ ] Revisé tercera persona: he/she/it + s.
* [ ] Revisé pasado: did + verb base en preguntas/negativas.
