3. Gramática esencial para llegar a B1
3.1 Present Simple
Úsalo para rutinas, hábitos, gustos, hechos generales y horarios.
Forma
Estructura
Ejemplo
Afirmativa
Subject + verb
I work in the morning.
He/She/It
verb + s/es
She studies English.
Negativa
don't/doesn't + verb base
I don't work on Sundays. / He doesn't drive.
Pregunta
Do/Does + subject + verb?
Do you like coffee? / Does she study?
Marcadores frecuentes: always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never, every day, on weekends.
Error común
Después de does/doesn't, el verbo vuelve a forma base.
Incorrecto: Does she studies?
Correcto: Does she study?
Práctica
1. Complete: My brother ___ (watch) series at night.
2. Negative: I play soccer on Sundays.
3. Question: She speaks English.
4. Answer: How often do you study English?
Clave
1. watches | 2. I don't play soccer on Sundays. | 3. Does she speak English? | 4. Ejemplo: I study English five times a week.
3.2 Present Continuous
Úsalo para acciones que están pasando ahora, situaciones temporales y planes ya organizados.
Uso
Ejemplo
Ahora
I am studying right now.
Temporal
I am working on a project this week.
Plan organizado
I am meeting my friend tomorrow.
Diferencia clave
Present Simple: I work every day. = rutina.
Present Continuous: I am working now. = en este momento o temporal.
3.3 Past Simple
Úsalo para acciones terminadas en el pasado. Es básico para B1 porque en speaking casi siempre te pedirán contar experiencias.
Tipo
Forma
Ejemplo
Regular
verb + ed
I visited my family yesterday.
Irregular
forma especial
I went to the mall.
Negativa
didn't + verb base
I didn't go out.
Pregunta
Did + subject + verb base?
Did you study last night?
Marcadores frecuentes: yesterday, last week, last month, ago, in 2024, when I was a child.
Base
Past
Ejemplo
go
went
I went to school.
do
did
I did my homework.
have
had
We had dinner.
make
made
She made a cake.
buy
bought
I bought a phone.
take
took
He took a bus.
see
saw
I saw a movie.
come
came
They came late.
eat
ate
We ate tacos.
get
got
I got home at 9.
Mini narración modelo
Last weekend, I stayed at home because I was tired. On Saturday, I watched a movie and studied English for one hour. On Sunday, I visited my family and we had lunch together. It was simple, but I enjoyed it.
3.4 Past Continuous
Úsalo para una acción que estaba en progreso en el pasado, especialmente cuando otra acción la interrumpe.
Estructura
Ejemplo
was/were + verb-ing
I was studying when my friend called.
while + past continuous
While I was walking, it started to rain.
3.5 Futuro: going to, will y present continuous
Forma
Uso principal
Ejemplo
be going to
planes e intenciones
I am going to study tonight.
will
decisiones rápidas, predicciones, promesas
I think I will pass the exam.
present continuous
planes organizados
I am taking the exam on Friday.
Para speaking
Si te preguntan: What are your plans for next year?
Respuesta B1: Next year, I am going to continue studying, and I want to improve my English because it can help me at school and work.
3.6 Modals: can, could, should, have to, must, may, might
Modal
Función
Ejemplo
can
habilidad/permiso
I can speak basic English.
could
pasado o petición amable
Could you repeat that, please?
should
consejo
You should practice every day.
have to
obligación externa
I have to study for my exam.
must
obligación fuerte
You must bring your ID.
may/might
posibilidad
I might travel next month.
Regla
Después de un modal, usa verbo base:
Correcto: I should study.
Incorrecto: I should to study / I should studying.

3.8 Comparatives and superlatives
Tipo
Regla
Ejemplo
Corto
adjective + er / the + adjective + est
fast, faster, the fastest
Largo
more / the most + adjective
more interesting, the most interesting
Irregular
good/better/best; bad/worse/worst
This book is better than that one.
Speaking útil
I prefer studying at night because it is quieter than studying in the afternoon. For me, listening is the most difficult skill, but speaking is the most important.
3.9 Present Perfect
Este tema suele separar A2 de B1. Úsalo para experiencias de vida, cambios y acciones recientes conectadas con el presente.
Uso
Estructura
Ejemplo
Experiencias
have/has + past participle
I have visited Cancun.
Nunca
have/has never + participle
I have never been to Canada.
Preguntas
Have/Has + subject + participle?
Have you ever taken an English exam?
Desde/hace
for + period / since + starting point
I have studied English for two months. / since January.
Ya/todavía
already / yet
I have already finished. / I haven't finished yet.
Diferencia con pasado simple
Past Simple: I went to Cancun in 2022. = tiempo terminado y específico.
Present Perfect: I have been to Cancun. = experiencia, no importa cuándo.
3.10 Gerunds and infinitives
Forma
Después de
Ejemplo
verb + ing
enjoy, avoid, finish, keep
I enjoy learning English.
to + verb
want, need, decide, plan, hope
I want to improve my speaking.
ambas en algunos casos
like, love, hate, prefer
I like studying / I like to study.
3.11 Conditionals básicos
Tipo
Uso
Estructura
Ejemplo
Zero conditional
verdades generales
If + present, present
If you heat water, it boils.
First conditional
posibilidad real futura
If + present, will + verb
If I study, I will pass.
3.12 Connectors para sonar B1
Función
Conectores
Ejemplo
Agregar
and, also, too, in addition
I study English, and I also practice listening.
Contrastar
but, however, although
I like English, but speaking is difficult.
Razón
because, since
I study because I need it for school.
Resultado
so, therefore
I was tired, so I went home.
Orden
first, then, after that, finally
First, I read. Then, I answer questions.
Opinión
I think, I believe, in my opinion
I think English is useful.
