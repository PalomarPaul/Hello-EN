5. Speaking: cómo responder como B1
Para el examen de ubicación, hablar con estructura vale mucho. No necesitas frases perfectas; necesitas responder, conectar ideas y dar ejemplos.
5.1 Fórmula de respuesta B1
Fórmula 4 pasos
1. Respuesta directa.
2. Razón.
3. Ejemplo o detalle personal.
4. Cierre sencillo.

Pregunta: Why are you studying English?
Respuesta: I am studying English because I want to improve my opportunities. For example, English can help me at school and in future jobs. Also, I want to understand videos and conversations better. That is why English is important for me.
5.2 Frases para ganar tiempo sin bloquearte
* Let me think for a second.
* That's an interesting question.
* I don't know the exact word, but I mean...
* Could you repeat the question, please?
* Can you say that again more slowly, please?
* I understand the idea, but I need a moment to answer.
* In my opinion...
* For example...
* Another reason is...
* To be honest...
5.3 Preguntas típicas y respuestas modelo
Pregunta
Respuesta modelo
Tell me about yourself.
My name is David. I am from Mexico, and I am a student. I am interested in technology and English. In my free time, I like watching videos, listening to music, and spending time with my family. Right now, I am studying English because I want to reach a B1 level.
What do you usually do on weekdays?
On weekdays, I usually wake up early and get ready for school or work. After that, I study, do homework, and practice English for a while. In the evening, I try to rest, watch something online, or talk with my friends.
What did you do last weekend?
Last weekend, I stayed at home most of the time. I studied a little, watched a movie, and helped with some things at home. On Sunday, I relaxed because I was tired. It was not very exciting, but it was a good weekend.
What are you going to do next year?
Next year, I am going to continue studying and improving my English. I also want to be more organized and learn more about technology. If I have time, I would like to travel or take another course.
Do you prefer studying alone or with other people?
I prefer studying alone when I need to concentrate because it is quieter. However, studying with other people is useful for speaking practice. I think both options are good, depending on the activity.
Describe a place you like.
I like quiet places where I can relax and talk with friends. For example, I enjoy going to a mall or a park because I can walk, eat something, and spend time without stress. A good place for me is clean, safe, and not too noisy.
What is difficult about learning English?
For me, the most difficult part is speaking because sometimes I understand the question, but I don't know how to answer quickly. Pronunciation and vocabulary can also be difficult. However, I think practice helps a lot.
What technology do you use every day?
I use my phone every day to communicate, study, and watch videos. I also use a computer for homework and projects. Technology is useful because it saves time, but sometimes it can be distracting.
What should people do to be healthy?
People should sleep well, drink water, and exercise. They should also eat more vegetables and avoid too much junk food. I think small habits are important because they are easier to maintain.
Have you ever traveled to another place?
Yes, I have traveled to different places in my country. I enjoy traveling because I can learn about new food, people, and places. I haven't visited many countries yet, but I would like to do it in the future.
5.4 Mini monólogos para practicar
* My daily routine
* A person I admire
* A place I want to visit
* My favorite food
* A problem I solved
* My plans for the future
* Why English is important
* Technology in my life
* A healthy lifestyle
* A movie or series I like
Instrucción
Elige un tema y habla 60-90 segundos. Usa esta estructura:
1. Introduction: I want to talk about...
2. Details: First..., Also..., For example...
3. Opinion: I think... because...
4. Closing: That's why...
5.5 Pronunciación mínima para el examen
No necesitas acento perfecto. Prioriza claridad, ritmo y palabras comunes.
Tema
Qué cuidar
Ejemplos
Terminación -ed
Tres sonidos aproximados: /t/, /d/, /id/
worked, played, visited
Tercera persona -s
No olvidarla en he/she/it
She works, he studies
Contracciones
Suenan más naturales
I'm, you're, don't, didn't, I've
Palabras funcionales
No pronuncies todo igual de fuerte
I want to study English.
Entonación
Sube un poco en yes/no questions
Do you like it?
